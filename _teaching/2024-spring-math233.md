Teaching assistant for MATH 233: Calculus 3 at UMass Amherst.

Topics covered include vectors and vector-valued functions, partial derivatives and the gradient, optimization of multivariable functions, multiple integrals (double and triple), and vector calculus including line integrals, surface integrals, Green's theorem, Stokes' theorem, and the Divergence theorem.

Responsibilities include leading discussion sections, holding office hours, grading assignments and exams.
